why this exists

I'm just a student. Like you. I've struggled with finding good, free, organized resources. Coaching centers charge too much. Most websites are cluttered or outdated. So I decided to build what I wished I had.

This platform is my sadaqah jariyah — a small thing that I hope keeps giving long after I'm gone. No ads. No paywalls. Just resources, organized with care, available to anyone who needs them.

If this helps you pass one exam, get into one university, or understand one topic — that's enough. That's the whole point.
